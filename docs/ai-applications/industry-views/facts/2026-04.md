## Semiconductors & Supply Chain

- **2026-04-22**: TSMC Technology Symposium confirmed **High-NA EUV not adopted through 2029**. Roadmap: **A14 (2028), A13/A12 (2029), A10 (2030)**; DRAM D0a 2027–2028. DRAM and logic are first HNA adopters, not memory. (Source: 2.Quick Take ASML - High-NA EUV adoption postponed.pdf)

- **2026-04-23**: Bernstein frames HNA/LNA equivalence: **1 HNA = 2 LNA** in exposure; ASML revenue per exposure = 2x — HNA delay is financially neutral over long arc. (Source: 2.Quick Take ASML.pdf)

- **2026-04-23**: NAND ASP improved **~70bps in FY1Q26**, **90–100% in FY2Q26**. Smartphone OEM pricing moved from **$0.12/GB → $0.25/GB**; eSSD negotiations at **$0.35–0.37/GB**. (Source: GF -SNDK 3Q26 Preview.pdf)

- **2026-04-23**: GF Securities: **KV-cache adds ~100 EB NAND demand in 2027** (≈7% of total), potentially doubling 2028. (Source: GF -SNDK 3Q26 Preview.pdf)

- **2026-04-23**: Intel server CPU supply less constrained than feared; ASIC/Networking ASP up **30% Q/Q** (~$325M contribution). GPU:CPU attach ratio compressing **1.8 → 1.3–1.4x**. (Source: INTEL_20260424_0406.pdf; JPM_Intel_Better_Q1_Q2.pdf)

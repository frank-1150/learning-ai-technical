# AI Industry Views

This section collects and organizes three types of AI industry content, tracking the formation and evolution of industry understanding.

## Structure

- **Industry Consensus** — Widely accepted views with timestamps
- **Professional Perspectives** — Views from specific individuals or organizations, with traceable sources
- **Facts Collection** — Ongoing accumulation of discrete facts that can be elevated to consensus
